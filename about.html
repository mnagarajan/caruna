About us

Who we are

Welcome to the CARUNA ( Civil services Associations Reach to sUpport in National disAsters).


This initiative aims to supplement national effort to tackle disasters by promoting knowledge sharing and collaboration as an aid to official efforts to handle emergency situations in Central Government and across all States.

This pandemic can only be defeated if all of “US” together pool in our vast networks, experience, resources and expertise , to support , not supplement the humongous efforts that both the Central and the State Governments are making in containing the pandemic through our association and other networks.

CARUNA , as the acronym suggests, is an All Civil Services Associations initiative to counter the pandemic, as no single organisation can defeat it single handedly.
