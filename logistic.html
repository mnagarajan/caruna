Logistics

Accommodation

Participants will be accomodated in Kalindi , Happy Valley, Mahanadi and Valley View (Indira Bhawan) Executive Hostels.

Transport

Academy would be providing transport for pick up from Dehradun(Airport/Railway Station)on arrival and for departure from LBSNAA upto Dehradun (Airport/Railway Station).

IT

Academy is Wi-Fi enabled.

Medical

Basic OPD medical facility is available in the Academy Dispensary.

Mess

Academy would be hosting your stay for 29th, 30th and 31st May. If you wish to arrive before 29th May and/or stay after the Reunion, the accomodation would be alloted subject to the availability and will be payble @ Rs. 750/- for room rent and Rs. 500/- for mess per individual per day.

Sports Complex

Academy has a state of the art sports facility and gymnasium.
